About Our Waste Management Initiative

Why Waste Management Matters

Waste management is a critical aspect of sustainable development. Proper segregation and disposal of waste can reduce environmental impact, promote recycling, and lead to a cleaner and healthier planet. Our project leverages machine learning to automatically classify waste as biodegradable, recyclable, or non-recyclable to simplify this process.

Our Objective

Our AI-based system provides an intuitive and efficient platform to identify waste categories using image classification. This not only raises awareness but also encourages responsible waste handling practices among individuals and communities.

Sustainability

Encouraging eco-friendly practices and reducing landfill overflow through smart classification.

Technology Driven

Powered by deep learning models to ensure accurate and quick waste identification.

Community Focus

Designed for public benefit and local bodies to streamline waste collection and sorting.
